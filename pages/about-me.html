About me

See my resume

Hi, my name is Marvin !
A recent computer engineering graduate from Epita. I am currently looking for a next role as a software developer.

My passion for frontend development stemmed from a deep love of sharing and showing my projects to friends and family. What will always have the biggest impact for the user (here my family) would be the interface. It is truly the display of all of the work behind an application and it also doesn't hurt that you are able to have a direct visual feedback of your work. And it's this positive feedback as well as a love of trying out interfaces and figuring out what works and what doesn't and for what reason, that I made the decision to specialize myself in frontend development.

I have utilized my skills through projects and internships. I used my knowledge of the design tool Figma and general UI knowledge to redesign the app I worked on in my SNCF internship but also for the different project I did. Of course my coding knowledge was of great importance to carry out work, especially for my internship at KPMG working on a workflow SaaS management solution where both visuals and performance was of great importance. I worked on project using React native, React, Python, HTML, CSS and JavaScript for my frontend work. But I also have knowledge in SQL, API building and databases that I acquired before my specialization and reinforced some in frontend projects by utilizing APIs. But there isn't only code in the life of an engineer and as such I also greatly worked on my interpersonal skills especially during my internship.

I am interested in learning more about frontend development be it new frameworks, best practices or consolidating my skills.

In my free time, I like to explore new parts of Paris, especially parks, assembling and painting plastic models and traveling.
